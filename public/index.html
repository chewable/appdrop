Host your Google AppEngine applications.

Or you can browse the applications we already host.

Confidence builder: AppDrop is a proof-of-concept, made to show that the AppEngine platform released by Google is not closed, and could easily be supported by other hosts. Unlike Google's project, we make no claims to be able to scale beyond a small amount of traffic. In fact, at AppDrop we reserve the right to shut the whole thing down any time we feel like it. We're running on EC2, so our virtual machine could just disappear at any time. We hope you enjoy AppDrop. If we see enough demand, we might decided to make serious go at it. Only time will tell.
